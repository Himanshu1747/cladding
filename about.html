About Us

The Company’s Engineering Facilities Provide Comprehensive Design & Drafting Services Supported By A Product Development Section Which Is Constantly Enhancing Quality And Product Efficiencies.

Our services

Provide Best Services Industries

ENGINEERING

The Company’s Engineering facilities provide comprehensive Design & Drafting services supported by a Product Development section which is constantly enhancing quality and product efficiencies. Engineers & Draftsmen use updated design software's like MBS & STAAD Pro and the Tekla Structures 3-D software for highly professionalized detailing to achieve efficient, optimum and accurate engineering solutions.

MANUFACTURING

The company’s manufacturing facilities are spread over an area of 4 acres in Sikri (North India), Housing Fabrication and other Production activities, besides storage of finished products. Our facilities are fully equipped to manufacture all PEB components in house, starting from Anchor bolts, Primary & Secondary Members, Roof and Cladding System, Gutters, Down take Pipes etc. Production capacity of plants is currently at 30,000 tons per annum, and can be enhanced further to meet the growing market demand.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

CLADDING PROJECTS is committed to maintain its quality policy : To provide high quality services/products that conform to contractual specifications in line with customer requirements. To increase customer confidence and loyalty leading to a greater market share through genuine commitment to quality. To commit, as a team, to the manufacture of high quality structures and provide the right products and services, at all times.

Project Management

We take the stress out of project management by overseeing every detail, including permits, inspections, and timeline adherence.

Our experienced project managers keep you informed and ensure that your PEB project is completed on schedule and within budget.

Customization and Flexibility

We understand that each project is unique. That's why we offer a high degree of customization, allowing you to choose from various materials, finishes, and architectural features.

Our flexible approach ensures that your PEB aligns perfectly with your business requirements.
