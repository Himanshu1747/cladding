What materials do you work with?

We work with a variety of metals, including steel, aluminum, copper, brass, and alloys. Our experienced team is proficient in handling different grades and types of metal to ensure the highest quality and performance of the parts we manufacture.

What is your production capacity?

Our production capacity depends on the complexity and quantity of the parts being manufactured. We have invested in state-of-the-art machinery and employ efficient production processes to ensure timely delivery without compromising on quality. For specific inquiries regarding production capacity, please contact our sales team.

Can you accommodate custom orders?

Yes, we specialize in custom metal part manufacturing. Whether you require modifications to existing designs or entirely new parts developed from scratch, our engineering team will work closely with you to understand your requirements and deliver tailor-made solutions that meet your needs.

What quality assurance do you have?

Quality is our top priority, and we adhere to stringent quality control measures throughout the manufacturing process. From material inspection and machining to finishing and final inspection, we maintain strict adherence to industry standards to ensure that every part meets the required specifications and exceeds customer expectations.

What is your lead time for orders?

Our lead time for orders varies depending on factors such as the complexity of the parts, order volume, and current production schedule. We strive to provide accurate lead time estimates for each order and work diligently to meet our customers' deadlines while maintaining the highest standards of quality.

How do you handle logistics?

We have established partnerships with reliable logistics providers to ensure safe and timely delivery of our products to customers worldwide. Whether you require local delivery or international shipping, we will coordinate the logistics to ensure that your order reaches you in optimal condition and within the agreed timeframe.
